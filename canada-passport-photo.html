Frequently asked questions

Is this size correct for a Canadian passport?

Yes – Canada requires a 50×70 mm photo with a 31–36 mm head height, which this tool crops to automatically.

Can a Canadian passport photo be taken at home?

Yes, you may supply your own photo if it meets the official specification. Many applicants print the result and have it verified at the counter.

What about the guarantor/date requirement?

Those are handled on the application, not on the photo itself. This tool ensures the image meets the size and quality specs.
