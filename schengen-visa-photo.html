Frequently asked questions

Which countries accept this photo?

The Schengen photo standard (35×45 mm, ICAO) is accepted by all Schengen states, including Germany, France, Italy, Spain, the Netherlands and more.

What head size does a Schengen visa photo need?

The head must be 32–36 mm from chin to crown, i.e. 70–80% of the photo height – which the tool measures and crops for you.
