Frequently asked questions

Can I use this photo for a US passport application?

Yes. The US State Department lets you supply your own photo as long as it meets the official 2×2 in specifications – which this tool checks for you. You can print it or upload the digital file for online applications.

Does it work for a US visa or ESTA?

Yes, US visa and ESTA use the same 2×2 in / 51×51 mm specification with a white background.

How do I print a 2x2 inch photo?

Download the print sheet and have it printed as a standard 4×6 in photo at any drugstore or photo lab – then cut out the 2×2 in photos.

Is my photo uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your photo never leaves your device.
